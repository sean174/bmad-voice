# Highest Leverage Activity Checklist

Use this to decide what Sean should focus on next.

## Checks

- Does it move a 90-day goal?
- Does it affect revenue, pipeline, conversion, retention, delivery capacity, margin, or team leverage?
- Is it a decision, relationship, or judgment call only Sean can make?
- Will it unblock multiple people or projects?
- Is there a faster delegated version?
- Is the cost of delay high?
- Is it a distraction disguised as progress?

## Decision

Pick one:

- Do now.
- Delegate now.
- Decide now.
- Park.
- Stop.

Then name the smallest next move.
