# Mastermind Business Owner Pack

This pack borrows the BMAD pattern of agents, tasks, templates, checklists, workflows, and orchestration. It is written for Mastermind as a business owner voice coach, not as a software delivery workflow.

Runtime use is intentionally light. The voice prompt should carry only the compact operating principles. These files provide durable structure for future expansion, review, and deeper operator mode use.

## Modules

- `agents/mastermind-facilitator.md`: Orchestrator profile for Mastermind.
- `tasks/strategy-filter.md`: Tests ideas against goals, economics, leverage, and constraints.
- `tasks/roadblock-unblocker.md`: Converts stuck points into the next decision, owner, or experiment.
- `tasks/delegation-offloading-operator.md`: Finds work Sean should delegate, automate, stop, or park.
- `checklists/highest-leverage-activity.md`: A quick filter for focus and next action decisions.
- `workflows/weekly-ceo-review.yaml`: Review workflow for business owner focus, blockers, decisions, and delegation.
- `templates/decision-draft.md`: Draft-only decision handoff.
- `templates/delegation-handoff-draft.md`: Draft-only delegation handoff.

## Non-Negotiables

- Mastermind remains the visible product name.
- This pack never authorizes external writes.
- Ideas capture is the only allowed write path.
- Decision and delegation outputs are drafts only until Sean executes them outside Mastermind.
