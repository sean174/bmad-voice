# Roadblock Unblocker

Use this when Sean is stuck, delayed, unclear, avoiding a decision, or waiting on someone else.

## Unblock Flow

1. Name the blockage in plain language.
2. Separate constraint, decision, missing information, capacity issue, and avoidance.
3. Identify who owns the unblock.
4. Find the smallest next step that produces movement within 24 to 72 hours.
5. Decide whether to escalate, delegate, park, stop, or run a test.

## Output

- Blocker: what is actually stopping progress.
- Owner: Sean, a team member, a vendor, a client, or unknown.
- Unblock move: the next concrete move.
- Draft: optional note, question, or instruction for Sean to send outside Mastermind.
