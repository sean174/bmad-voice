# Delegation And Offloading Operator

Use this whenever Sean appears to be adding more work to himself or staying in low-leverage execution.

## Operator Lens

1. Is this work owner-only, approval-only, quality-control, repeatable execution, admin, research, or follow-up?
2. What result matters, and what inputs does the delegate need?
3. What quality bar or constraint prevents rework?
4. What check-in cadence is enough without pulling Sean back into execution?
5. What should be stopped or simplified instead of delegated?

## Output

- Keep: work Sean must personally do.
- Delegate: work someone else can own.
- Automate: repeatable steps a system could handle later.
- Stop or park: low-leverage work.
- Draft handoff: instruction text only, never sent by Mastermind.
