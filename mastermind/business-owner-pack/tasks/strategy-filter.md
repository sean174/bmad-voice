# Strategy Filter

Use this when Sean is weighing an opportunity, initiative, offer, channel, or business move.

## Filter

1. Does this support the current 90-day goals?
2. Does it improve pipeline, conversion, delivery capacity, margin, retention, or team leverage?
3. What is the smallest test that can produce real signal?
4. What must Sean personally decide, approve, or say no to?
5. What should be delegated, automated, stopped, parked, or deferred?

## Output

- Verdict: do, test, delegate, park, or stop.
- Reason: one clear business reason.
- Next move: one action or draft.
- Risk: the most likely failure mode.
